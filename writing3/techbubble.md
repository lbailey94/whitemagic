# DOTCOM vs AI BUBBLES:

This is a marathon, not a sprint!

> **Common DNA:** positive gross margins, repeat-use product, sticky ecosystem, and just-enough capital.

5. **Look for data or distribution moats.** 

Proprietary structured datasets, regulatory approvals, or embedded enterprise contracts beat bare-model startups.
