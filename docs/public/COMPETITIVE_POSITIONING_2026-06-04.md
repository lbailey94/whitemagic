- **AI-CONSTITUTION**: Has 9 Laws with falsification protocols and 426 tests. More comprehensive for *prohibitions* but less dynamic for *side-effect tracking*.

**Verdict**: Conceptually strong. Needs published specification and benchmark.

## Honest Assessment

WhiteMagic is **conceptually ahead and practically behind.**

The ideas are prescient and in some cases unique (28 Gana, 5D coordinates, polyglot breadth, prescience track record). The execution is a research artifact with 2,379 tests but no public UI, no published benchmarks, and a community of one.

The competitors are shipping production systems with dashboards, managed services, and published papers. They are not *better* — they are *different* — but they are *available*.

The window for establishing WhiteMagic as a category leader is narrowing. The next 30 days should focus on **publication, not implementation**.
