## 0. Executive Summary

**Cannot incorporate for free in Georgia.** The state charges a mandatory $100 filing fee for online LLC formation. However, formation services charge $0 — you only pay the state fee. Total minimum cash outlay for a fully operational LLC with bank account: **$100–$200** (depending on bank choice).

**Fastest path**: File online yourself → get EIN same day → open Axos or Mercury account (both $0 minimum) → submit SFF Rolling Application within **48 hours**.

## 2. EIN (Employer Identification Number)

| Attribute | Detail |
|-----------|--------|
| **Cost** | **$0** |
| **Time** | 5–10 minutes online, EIN issued immediately |
| **Where** | [irs.gov/ein](https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/apply-for-an-employer-identification-number-ein-online) |
| **Prerequisite** | LLC must be filed with state first (IRS requires entity to exist) |
| **Availability** | Mon–Fri 6am–1am ET, Sat 6am–9pm ET, Sun 6pm–12am ET |
| **Limit** | 1 EIN per responsible party per day |

**Critical:** Do NOT use third-party EIN services. The IRS gives EINs away for free. Any site charging for an EIN is a scam or unnecessary middleman.

### 3.2 Detailed Analysis

#### Mercury (Recommended for Tech/Grant Context)
- **$0 minimum deposit, $0 monthly fee, $0 overdraft fees**
- Unlimited transactions, virtual + physical debit cards
- Free domestic wires, mid-4% yield on treasury accounts
- Strong API for integrations (useful if you automate financial tracking)
- No cash deposits (not an issue for grant-funded research)
- Apply online in 10 minutes
- **No promo bonus currently**
- **FDIC insured** via Choice Financial Group and Column N.A.

#### Novo (Recommended for Simple Operations)
- **$0 minimum deposit, $0 monthly fee**
- Unlimited transactions, free invoicing and budgeting tools
- 40+ business integrations (QuickBooks, Stripe, etc.)
- No cash deposits
- ATM fee reimbursement up to $7/month
- Apply online, setup in a few days
- **No promo bonus currently**
- **FDIC insured** via Middlesex Federal Savings

#### Truist Simple Business Checking (Recommended if You Want Branch Access + Bonus)
- **$100 minimum opening deposit**
- **$0 monthly maintenance fee**
- 50 combined transactions/month free ($0.50 each over)
- $2,000 cash deposits/month free ($0.0031 per dollar over)
- **PROMO: $400 bonus** if you open by 7/9/2026 using code `SB26Q2CHECKING` and deposit $2,000+ within 30 days
- Physical branches (if you ever need to walk in)
- Requires LLC + EIN
- **FDIC insured**

#### Axos Basic Business Checking (Recommended for Zero-Barrier Entry)
- **$0 minimum deposit, $0 monthly fee**
- Unlimited transactions
- Free domestic incoming wires, 2 free domestic outgoing wires
- **PROMO: Up to $200 cashback** if you maintain $3,000 avg daily balance for first few months (code `START200`, expires 6/30/2026)
- No cash deposits (online-only)
- Entirely online application
- **FDIC insured**

### 3.3 Recommendation for WhiteMagic Labs

**Primary choice: Mercury or Novo**
- Zero cost to open
- No monthly fees ever
- Unlimited transactions (no risk of overage during grant deposit/withdrawal)
- Online-only is fine — you don't handle cash
- Both have clean APIs if you ever want to automate

**Secondary choice: Truist Simple**
- If you want the $400 promo bonus (effectively reduces your LLC cost to negative $300)
- If you value having a physical branch nearby
- But you need $100 to open + $2,000 to trigger the bonus

**Avoid:** Capital One Business Basic ($15/month unless you maintain $2K), Bank of America (fees after 12 months), Chase (minimum balance games).

## 6. Grant-Specific Considerations

### SFF Rolling Application Requirements
- ✅ LLC required
- ✅ Bank account required (must be in business name with EIN)
- ✅ No academic affiliation required
- ✅ MIT + CC-BY license already matches
- ⏳ Timeline: 6–8 months evaluation, funding likely Q1 2027

### Schmidt Sciences RFP Considerations
- LLC is **not strictly required** for individual PI, but incorporation strengthens application
- Bank account needed for fund disbursement
- Academic / non-profit affiliation is **strongly preferred** — LLC alone may not satisfy this
- Consider finding a fiscal sponsor (university, research institute) if no academic affiliation

### Fiscal Sponsor Alternative
If academic affiliation is a blocker for Schmidt:
- **Option A:** Partner with a university lab as co-PI
- **Option B:** Use a fiscal sponsor like [Open Collective](https://opencollective.com/) or a research foundation
- **Option C:** Apply as individual PI and explicitly note the LLC structure in the application
- Fiscal sponsor typically charges 5–10% administrative fee on grant funds
