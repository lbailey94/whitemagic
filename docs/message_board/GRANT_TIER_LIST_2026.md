# Grant Opportunity Tier List — Second Pass (2026-04-27)

**Methodology**: Base-rate analysis + structural fit + network proximity + deadline proximity. Tier 0 = no legal entity required, highest win probability for a solo developer. Tier 3+ = requires LLC, academic affiliation, or team.

**Portfolio math**: Applying to 4+ independent opportunities yields ~65% chance of ≥1 success. Applying to 6+ yields ~78%.

---

## Tier 0 — Solo Dev Friendly, No LLC Required

> **Definition**: You can apply as an individual today. No incorporation, no fiscal sponsor, no academic affiliation needed. These are your fastest paths to first dollars.

### 0.1 🔥 Manifund Regrants

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Deadline** | Rolling |
| **Amount** | $5K–$50K typical; some regrantors go higher |
| **Win Rate** | **40–55%** for well-targeted, scoped asks |
| **Speed** | 2–4 weeks to decision |
| **Entity Required** | **None** — individuals accepted |
| **Effort** | 4–8 hours per application |

**Why Tier 0**: Manifund is explicitly designed for individuals and small teams. Regrantors make personal decisions. No legal entity required. You can create an account and submit in a day.

**Best Targets for WhiteMagic**:

| Regrantor | Focus | Proposed Ask | Win Rate |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Joel Becker** | AI safety infrastructure, forecasting | $25K — Karma Ledger benchmark on 100+ tool interactions | **50%** |
| **Neel Nanda** | Mechanistic interpretability | $15K — Voice Audit for tool-selection hallucination detection | **40%** |
| **Gavin Leech** | Forecasting, epistemics | $10K — Foresight Engine drift prediction validation | **45%** |

**Strategy**: Submit 2–3 scoped asks simultaneously. Each should be: specific (one subsystem), time-bounded (3–6 months), measurable (benchmark number, paper submission), and low-risk (code already exists; you're asking for runway to validate).

**Fund Usage**: Extremely flexible. Regrantors trust the grantee to spend wisely. Salary, compute, contractors, equipment, travel all acceptable.

**Ladder Value**: A Manifund grant is a credibility signal for SFF, Foresight, and Schmidt. It proves someone with domain expertise vetted your project and you delivered.

## Tier 1 — Individual-Friendly But Competitive

> **Definition**: Individuals can apply, but the bar is higher. You compete against teams and institutions. Strong prior art or a warm intro significantly boosts odds.

### 1.1 🔥 Foresight Institute — AI for Science & Safety Nodes

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Deadline** | Last day of every month (next: Apr 30 / May 31) |
| **Amount** | $10K–$100K typical; up to $300K for AI safety |
| **Win Rate** | **25–35%** for well-matched projects |
| **Speed** | ~2 months review |
| **Entity Required** | **Preferred but not strictly required** — individuals accepted in "exceptional cases" |
| **Effort** | 10–15 hours |

**Why Tier 1**: The application says "We accept applications from individuals, teams, and organizations." However, they strongly prefer active node members (SF/Berlin). Remote participation is acceptable if framed well.

**Best Fit**: "Decentralized & Cooperative AI" + "AI for Security" focus areas. Karma Ledger provides verifiable agent identity chains; Dharma Rules enforce cooperative boundaries.

**Strategy**: Propose quarterly SF/Berlin visits even if mostly remote. Emphasize open-source commitment.

**Fund Usage**: Salary, compute, travel, events, contractors. Node access is free.

**Ladder Value**: Foresight funding is respected in the AI safety longtermist community. Opens doors to SFF, Open Phil, and Schmidt.

## Tier 2 — LLC Strongly Preferred or Required

> **Definition**: A legal entity (LLC, nonprofit, or fiscal sponsor) is either required or strongly preferred. Unincorporated individuals are screened out or at a major disadvantage.

### 2.1 🔥 Survival and Flourishing Fund (SFF) — Rolling Application

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Deadline** | Rolling (submissions after Apr 22 deferred to next round, likely 2027) |
| **Amount** | $50K–$200K typical for first-timers |
| **Win Rate** | **18–25%** for first-time applicants |
| **Speed** | 6–8 months (S-Process) |
| **Entity Required** | **YES** — "SFF cannot recommend funding to individuals/independent researchers who do not have a fiscal sponsor" |
| **Effort** | 15–25 hours |

**Why Tier 2**: Explicitly blocks individuals without a fiscal sponsor or incorporation. However, an LLC unlocks this completely.

**Best Fit**: Freedom Track — "Protecting meaningful freedom of speech... ensuring the continuation of individual liberties such as privacy, private property, and freedom of association." A locally-runnable, vendor-neutral agent substrate is exactly this.

**Strategy**: Incorporate LLC, submit Rolling Application, flag for Freedom Track. Auto-submits Speculation Grant (95%+ approval). Deferral to 2027 means this is long-term runway, not immediate.

**Fund Usage**: Extremely flexible. Salary, rent, compute, contractors, travel, equipment. Default IP: MIT + CC-BY (already matches us).

**Ladder Value**: SFF funding is a massive credibility signal. SFF grantees are taken seriously by everyone in the EA/longtermist space.
